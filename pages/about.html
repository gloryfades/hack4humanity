Catholic Charitiesâ€™ Baby Steps program provides in-home parent/child attachment and parenting
support to pregnant women and infants, particularly those who have been impacted by the criminal
justice system. Baby Steps staff works to concentrate on early attachment and those elements of
early childhood that can be of concern to new mothers such as maternal depression, anxiety and
