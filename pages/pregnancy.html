Whether you are thinking about or are in the middle of pregnancy, there are a variety of actions you can do to prepare yourself to be as healthy as possible for your baby. Here is a general guideline to consider before conception.

Health and Nutrition

Now that you know you are pregnant or wish to become pregnant, it is extremely important to care for yourself both mentally and physically. By following the guidelines below, you'll have a better chance of having a problem-free pregnancy and a healthy baby.

Seek medical attention

If you lack insurance, don't worry! There are numerous sites in the area that help uninsured and low-income people by offering examinations at a reduced price or for free.

  • Alexian Clinic
  • Alviso Health Center
  • Valley Speciality Center
  • City Ministries
  • Valley Health Center

Mental Wellness

Although pregnancy is a joyous miracle, it is not unusual that a woman finds this transition in her life to be stressful. Some women may develop mental health issues during the onset of pregnancy while others who previously had problems are at high risk of becoming unwell after childbirth according to the

Royal College of Psychiatrists
